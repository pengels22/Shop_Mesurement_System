Calibration Summary

Current build uses the backend calibration profile for saved measurements.

  • Measurements are validated at a minimum of 1 mm.
  • Display and save values are in inches only.
  • Masking and perspective files are stored in Core/config.

Current Workflow

  1. Open the measurement page and capture a frame.
  2. Add or adjust X, Y, Z, and Mx lines.
  3. Save the part into the active project.
  4. Review saved parts in the parts browser.
